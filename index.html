How to Play?

  1. Click Here for tutorial to how to play the game.
  2. Press "Any Key/Button" to start the game.
  3. Among the 4 coloured tiles one tile will blink and you have to remeber that tile.
  4. After the blink you have to click the same tile that was blinked.
  5. If you clciked the right tile, some other tile will blink then you have to click the tiles oin sequence to move to next level.
  6. For example: If green tile blinks first you will click the green tile and then in next level if yellow tile blinks then first "Green" then "Yellow" tile should be clicked.
